# Beforest Overview

## What Beforest Says It Does

Beforest describes itself as creating forest-friendly collectives that restore ecological balance through permaculture, regenerative farming, and community-led living.

Key themes from the homepage:

- consciously connecting soil, water, roots, people, and wildlife
- reimagining community living
- creating community-owned and managed collectives
- building regenerative food forests

## Core Philosophy

Beforest says its landscape design is inspired by forests and uses permaculture as the framework for long-term ecological solutions.

The site emphasizes:

- self-regulating farming systems
- groundwater recharge
- soil health
- biodiversity increase
- coexistence with wildlife
- collaboration with local communities and experts

## Ownership And Community Model

According to the homepage snapshot:

- collectives are crowdfunded and co-owned by members
- each member privately owns a part of the landscape and collectively owns the entire expanse
- Beforest typically uses a Limited Liability Partnership model in which members are owners of the land and Beforest acts as the enabler

## How Beforest Describes The Difference

The site frames the offer as a farm life, not just a farmhouse.

Members are expected to participate in:

- farming and conservation activity
- landscape growth with friends and family
- learning permaculture growing methods
- community-led decision making
