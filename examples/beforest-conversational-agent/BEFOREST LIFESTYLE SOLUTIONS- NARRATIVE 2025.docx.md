**6\. What We Do**

We build regenerative living communities that restore landscapes and demonstrate new models for sustainable prosperity.

Our approach combines:

1. **Ecological Design:**  
   We plan landscapes holistically — restoring soil, water, and biodiversity systems while integrating human settlements harmoniously within them.  
2. **Collective Ownership & Stewardship:**  
   Each Beforest community operates as a collective, where residents share responsibility for the land and contribute to long-term restoration.  
3. **Community Regeneration:**  
   We empower local communities through jobs, enterprises, and knowledge-sharing in agroforestry, conservation, and ecosystem management.  
4. **Regenerative Enterprises:**  
   We build viable livelihood models — from agroforestry produce to eco-tourism and education — that create continuous financial and social returns.

**8\. Our Approach: The 4 Returns Framework in Practice**

Beforest applies the **4 Returns Framework** — a proven approach to holistic landscape restoration that delivers:

* **Return of Inspiration:** Reconnecting people to the land and a shared purpose.  
* **Social Return:** Building cohesive communities and local capacities.  
* **Natural Return:** Restoring ecosystems, water cycles, and biodiversity.  
* **Financial Return:** Creating long-term economic and livelihood security.

Each Beforest collective becomes a *living lab* for regeneration — blending design, learning, and practice to create measurable returns across all four dimensions.

**9\. Our Story**

Beforest Lifestyle Solutions began with a simple yet radical idea — that human settlements can restore nature rather than harm it.  
Founded by a group of forward looking entrepreneurs, conservation minded people and dreamers, Beforest set out to create communities where living regeneratively was both desirable and achievable.

Starting with degraded lands in Coorg, Telangana, and Other parts of India we worked to restore water systems, revive forests, and grow livelihoods and demonstrate replicable models. Each project became a model of what’s possible when ecology guides economy and community action.

Today, Beforest continues to pioneer regenerative lifestyles across India — bridging urban aspiration with rural regeneration. Each landscape we restore brings us closer to a future where **the way we live is also the way we heal the planet**.
